Octa.Space

distributed computing, VPN and data storage

OctaSpace is a cloud platform that allows you to access distributed computing, data storage and serving, VPN, and more. It's designed to be easy to use, even for those who are new to the concept of distributed computing. With OctaSpace, you can tap into the power of CPU and GPU resources from nodes all over the world to tackle heavy tasks. And if you have spare hardware, you can even become a host and earn by renting out your CPU/GPU or traffic. Unleash the power of distributed computing, secure your data with our VPN, and store your files with ease.
