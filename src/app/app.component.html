Octa.Space

distributed computing, VPN and data storage

OctaSpace is a user-friendly cloud platform that grants access to distributed computing, data storage, serving, VPN, and more. Whether you're a beginner or seasoned in distributed computing, OctaSpace makes it easy for you. Tap into the combined power of CPU and GPU resources from nodes worldwide to tackle even the most challenging tasks. You can also become a host and earn rewards by renting out your spare CPU/GPU or traffic. Secure your data with our VPN services and enjoy effortless file storage.
